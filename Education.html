    Organizations Providing Free Education



    🌟 Khan Academy: Free Learning Platform


    📚 About Khan Academy

    Khan Academy is a non-profit organization providing free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. Founded by Sal Khan in 2008, it offers courses in various subjects like Math, Science, Computing, Economics, and more.

    🔑 Key Features

    • ✔️ Free Courses & Interactive Learning
    • ✔️ Personalized Learning Dashboard
    • ✔️ Available in 50+ Languages
    • ✔️ Trusted by NASA, MIT, and More

    📖 Subjects Offered

    • 📌 Mathematics (Basic to Advanced Calculus)
    • 📌 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering)
    • 📌 Computer Science (Programming, AI, Data Structures)
    • 📌 Economics & Finance (Micro & Macro Economics)
    • 📌 Test Prep (SAT, MCAT, LSAT, IIT JEE, GMAT)

    🌍 Learn Anytime, Anywhere

    Khan Academy allows self-paced learning for students, teachers, and lifelong learners.


    🌟 PM eVidya: One Nation, One Digital Platform


    📚 About PM eVidya

    PM eVidya is a government initiative aimed at providing digital education to students across India through multiple platforms, including TV channels, radio, and online learning portals.

    🔑 Key Features

    • ✔️ One Nation, One Digital Platform
    • ✔️ DIKSHA: Free e-Learning Portal
    • ✔️ Dedicated TV Channels for Classes 1-12 (Swayam Prabha)
    • ✔️ Community Radio & Podcasts for Learning

    📖 Educational Resources

    • 📌 DIKSHA Portal (E-books, Video Lessons, Assessments)
    • 📌 Swayam Prabha (24x7 Educational TV Channels)
    • 📌 ePathshala (NCERT Digital Library)
    • 📌 Community Radio (Audio Learning Content)

    🌍 Accessible Learning for All

    PM eVidya ensures inclusive education by making digital content available to students in remote areas through TV, radio, and online platforms.


    🌟 NPTEL: National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning


    📚 About NPTEL

    NPTEL is an initiative by the IITs and IISc to offer free online courses in engineering, science, management, and humanities. It provides certification and high-quality education for learners worldwide.

    🔑 Key Features

    • ✔️ Free Online Courses by IITs & IISc
    • ✔️ Video Lectures & Assignments
    • ✔️ Certification on Exam Completion
    • ✔️ Industry-Relevant Content

    📖 Subjects Offered

    • 📌 Engineering (Computer Science, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, etc.)
    • 📌 Science (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology)
    • 📌 Management (Finance, Marketing, Operations)
    • 📌 Humanities (Economics, Psychology, English Literature)

    🌍 Learn Anytime, Anywhere

    NPTEL provides flexible learning with video lectures and assignments for students, professionals, and educators.


    🌟 Teach For India: Quality Education for All


    📚 About Teach For India

    Teach For India is a non-profit organization that aims to provide an excellent education to all children by recruiting and training young leaders to teach in underprivileged schools.

    🔑 Key Features

    • ✔️ Fellowship Program for Young Leaders
    • ✔️ Focus on Leadership & Educational Reform
    • ✔️ Works with Underprivileged Schools
    • ✔️ Strong Alumni Network Driving Change

    📖 How You Can Help

    • 📌 Join as a Fellow (Teach in Schools for 2 Years)
    • 📌 Donate (Support Education Initiatives)
    • 📌 Volunteer (Be Part of the Change)
    • 📌 Spread Awareness (Advocate for Education)

    🌍 Transforming Education in India

    Teach For India is creating a movement of leaders committed to ending educational inequity in India.


    🌟 Pratham Education Foundation: Education for Every Child


    📚 About Pratham

    Pratham Education Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the quality of education in India by providing innovative learning programs for children and youth.

    🔑 Key Features

    • ✔️ Focus on Foundational Learning & Literacy
    • ✔️ Digital Learning & Skill Development Programs
    • ✔️ Early Childhood Education & Remedial Programs
    • ✔️ Research-Backed Teaching Methods

    📖 Programs Offered

    • 📌 ASER (Annual Status of Education Report) - National Education Survey
    • 📌 Hybrid Learning - Digital Education for All
    • 📌 Vocational Training - Job-Ready Skill Development
    • 📌 Community-Based Learning - Empowering Rural Areas

    🌍 Transforming Education in India

    Pratham is working towards ensuring that every child in India has access to quality education and a bright future.


    🌟 Room to Read: Transforming Education for Children


    📚 About Room to Read

    Room to Read is a global nonprofit organization focused on improving literacy and gender equality in education for children across the world. They develop literacy skills and a habit of reading among primary school children and support girls to complete secondary school with the skills necessary to negotiate key life decisions.

    🔑 Key Features

    • ✔️ Literacy Programs
    • ✔️ Girls’ Education Programs
    • ✔️ Local Language Book Publishing
    • ✔️ Community Engagement in Education

    📖 Educational Resources

    • 📌 Early Literacy Programs (Building literacy skills in primary school children)
    • 📌 Girls’ Education Programs (Supporting girls to complete secondary education)
    • 📌 Library Programs (Providing access to books and learning resources)
    • 📌 Local Language Publishing (Creating books in local languages for children)

    🌍 Empowering Children and Communities

    Room to Read works to create a world where all children can achieve their full potential and lead productive lives. Their programs focus on improving educational outcomes and gender equality in communities across Asia and Africa.
