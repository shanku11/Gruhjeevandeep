    National entrance exam for medical courses like MBBS and BDS in India.

    Official Website:

    UPSC Civil Services

    One of the toughest exams for recruitment into IAS, IPS, IFS, and other central services.

    Includes preliminary, mains, and interview rounds.

    Official Website:

    Conducted for various administrative posts in Telangana state government.

    Includes different group-level exams for various services.

    Official Website:

    APPSC Groups

    Similar to TSPSC but for Andhra Pradesh state government posts.

    Includes Group I, II, III, and IV exams.

    Official Website:

    GATE

    Entrance exam for M.Tech admissions and PSU jobs.

    Official Website:
