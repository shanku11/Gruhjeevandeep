Telangana Govt Websites Directory

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

Overview: Financial inclusion scheme for accessing banking, savings, credit, and pension.

  • Zero minimum balance account.
  • Overdraft facility after 6 months.
Apply Now

Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

Overview: Pension scheme for the unorganized sector with guaranteed pension from age 60.

  • Flexible monthly contributions.
  • Tax benefits under Section 80CCD.
Apply Now

CM Relief Fund

Overview: Financial assistance for individuals in emergency situations (medical, disasters).

  • Immediate support for major medical treatments.
Apply Now

Rythu Bandhu Scheme

Overview: Direct investment support for farmers in Telangana to purchase inputs.

  • Ensures timely sowing and boosts productivity.
Apply Now

PM-KISAN Scheme

Overview: ₹6,000 per year direct income assistance to farmers across India.
